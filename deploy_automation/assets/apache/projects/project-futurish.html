Futurish Podcast

Futurish is a hobby project to maintain a long-distance friendship with my good friend Peter, who is from Melbourne, Australia. We met at Crytek and managed to even live in the same city (Melbourne) for a while. We sit down once a month to talk about topics ranging from technology and life in the digital age to education, whilst we try to maintain some sort of credibility.

  • Editor
